Diamond Culet

The cone-shaped bottom-most part of the diamond is the culet of it. A culet can be flat-surfaced or pointed based on the choice of the diamond cutter and sometimes based on the culture of the target market for the gemstone. Pointed culet is usually preferred, but it is susceptible to damage due to the sharpness at the end. While experts continue to debate whether a culet should there in a diamond, it is left to individual customers to make their choice.
