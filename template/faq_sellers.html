FAQs

For Sellers

1.As a seller, how do I benefit through IFtoSI?
Today, having an online counterpart to your brick-and-mortar store is a must – especially for diamond jewellery retailers. It takes real work to develop and maintain a successful site and this is where IFtoSI can help you in saving your cost and time. IFtoSI becomes your online storefront exposing you to a wide range of consumers directly who could be looking for products like yours.
IFtoSI is not a replacement for your brick and mortar store, rather it supports your brand online by increasing your reach and offering an additional revenue stream for your products. Most importantly it connects you directly with the end consumers and can effectively help you in trend forecasting, generating customer insights and feedback. For new brand entrants, it helps in creating awareness and raising your credibility online.
2.How do I offer my merchandise for display at IFtoSI?
In order to offer your merchandise for display at IFtoSI, you need to first become a member. IFtoSI membership is open and accessible for all reputable retailers and manufacturers. All applicants need to go through the accreditation process, which consists of:
1. Filling out the applications form
2. Agreeing to IFtoSI Terms & Conditions
3. Paying the Membership Fee
3.Do we need to submit any documents while applying for membership?
An IFtoSI applicant can apply for membership with uploading the following documents:
* Copy of any trade license / VAT certificate
* Proof of membership to local reputable Jewellery association,
diamond bourse (s),associated organisations;
* Catalogue of products 
* Duly completed application form
* Any other documents that may be deemed relevant by IFtoSI
4.How long is my membership valid?
Signing of the IFtoSI membership needs to be renewed annually. This means every year you will have to apply for a renewal.
6.What does this membership fee include?
This fee includes the accreditation of the company and the use of the IFtoSI title according to the licensing guidelines. It also includes clerical work and inclusion of promoting the company / store(s) in IFtoSI database and website.
7.How does selling on IFtoSI work once I become a member?
Selling on IFtoSI is easy. List the products you want to sell at IFtoSI. Customer sees your product and books an order. You and the customer then mutually decide upon the delivery mode and mode of payment.
8.What are my obligations as a seller?
You need to provide true, accurate, current and complete information about yourself as prompted by IFtoSI's membership form. IFtoSI reserves the right to confirm and validate the information and other details provided by you at any point of time. If you provide any information that is untrue (wholly or partly), inaccurate, incomplete, or not current or if IFtoSI has reasonable grounds to suspect that such information is untrue, inaccurate, and not current or not in accordance with this User Agreement, we have the right to indefinitely suspend or terminate your membership and and debar you from using the services of www.IFtoSI.com without prior notice whatsoever.
