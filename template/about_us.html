Why IFtoSI?
IFtoSI is the only platform in the world where the end buyer meets the first seller. Usually, a solitaire changes two to three hands before it comes into the hands of the end buyer. IFtoSI will help you connect with the manufacturer / direct importer who is sitting in the diamond bourse in Mumbai or any other part of the world ready to assist you to make your purchase. Not only you get your diamonds from a very genuine source but also you stand to save a lot of money. IFtoSI becomes an open marketplace with various sellers giving their best prices. The buyer always stands to gain in this scenario. All the companies listed on the IFtoSI website are well managed and professionally run companies. They are listed with various diamond bourses and have offices in many countries. Only companies which are registered and compliant with all government and council formalities are listed on IFtoSI. Websites which say ‘No middlemen involved’ is a misnomer as they themselves are the middleman. At IFtoSI we connect you directly with dealers and we are not involved with the transaction. Once you register, you are free to connect with anybody directly. Solitaires are valued and sold based on the GIA, IGI and HRD certificate. Buyers can match the certificate number with the laser inscription on the girdle of the diamond. Your solitaire certificate number can also be checked on certification laboratory website.
