About IFtoSI

IFtoSI is an online marketplace for your diamond solitaires, fine jewellery and bullion purchases. Our mission is to connect the 'end buyer with the 'first seller', thus providing the consumer with a phenomenal price edge vis-a-vis the traditional brick-and-mortar and online stores. With diamonds being our prominent product category, we named ourselves IFtoSI, which is an acronym for 'Internally Flawless' to 'Slight Inclusions', the grading terminology used while measuring the diamond clarity.

At IFtoSI, we're committed to offering you an exciting product selection, shopping convenience, exceptional service and most of all, unmatched prices! Our exhaustive list of sellers including diamond dealers, jewellery retailers and bullion merchants spread locally in India and across the globe who are specialists in their respective markets. So whether you are looking for a solitaire engagement ring, or a 2 carat round brilliant cut diamond, a studded white gold necklace or gold coins, IFtoSI is the place to be!
